    PURPOSE

    The intention of this website is to broadcast all of my interests & passions to my viewers. I hope that my viewers, from friends & family to random people who stumbled upon this website (hello!), can gain something after browsing. Whether that something is finding a beauty product to try out, learning a new way to organize jewelry, or even simply discovering a link to donate to significant charities. This website is not only a way for myself to lay out all my ideas into one designated area but also to reach out to others & demonstrate what the impact of a more creative & cleaner environment can do to an individual! Enjoy! :)
